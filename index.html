The Spirit Bear

The Spirit Bear is majestic bear, called the spirit bear due to its white fur, not to get confused with the polar bear. The spirit bear was put on the endangered species list on 2010. These Spirit Bears are black bear with a recessive gene. All of its exterior extremities are also lighter in color, but it is not considered albino.

This spirit bear population is on the steady decline. They are threatened by trophy hunters that want a chance to be able to hunt the illusive spirit bear. We chose this specific topic because, the spirit bear is a great and majestic animal, they are on the endangered species list. They are a symbol of the wonderful and lushes west coast. We hope that you learned something and say how majestic these bears are, and if you get a chance try and see one in nature it will be a life changing event in your life.
